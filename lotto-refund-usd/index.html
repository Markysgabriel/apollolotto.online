Refund Policy

About this Policy

This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which customers may request a refund for digital products and online courses purchased through our platform. Please read this document carefully before completing your purchase.

Refund Eligibility

We offer a full refund within 14 days of the original purchase date, no questions asked. To be eligible, your refund request must be submitted within this 14-day window from the date of your transaction.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, please contact our support team within the eligible period. Your request must include:

  • Your order number or transaction ID.
  • The email address used at checkout.
  • A brief description of the reason for the request (optional).

Refund Processing

Once your refund request is approved, the amount will be returned to your original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider, but typically take between 5 to 10 business days to reflect on your statement.

Requests Outside the Refund Window

Refund requests submitted after the 14-day period from the purchase date will not be honored. We encourage all customers to review the product details carefully before completing their purchase.

Non-Refundable Situations

The following situations are not eligible for a refund:

  • Requests submitted after the 14-day refund window.
  • Purchases where the digital content has been fully downloaded or consumed beyond reasonable use.
  • Cases of violation of our Terms and Conditions.

Customer Acknowledgment

By completing a purchase, the customer acknowledges and agrees to the terms outlined in this Refund Policy. This policy applies exclusively to digital products and online courses sold through this platform.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our platform after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact & Support

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact our support team at supportapollo98@gmail.com with the subject "Refund Request". Include your order number and the email address used at checkout. We are committed to ensuring a fair and transparent experience for every customer.
